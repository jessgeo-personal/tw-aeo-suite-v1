AEO Audit Bot (AIOptimize Bot)

About

The AEO Audit Bot is an automated crawler operated by Thatworkx Solutions that analyzes websites for Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). This bot helps website owners optimize their content for AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews.

User Agent

Mozilla/5.0 (compatible; AIOptimizeBot/1.0; +https://aeo.thatworkx.com/aeo-bot.html)

Purpose

  • Analyze technical foundation (schema markup, HTML structure)
  • Evaluate content structure for AI readability
  • Assess E-E-A-T signals
  • Check AI visibility factors
