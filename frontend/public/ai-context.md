# AIOptimize by Thatworkx - Core Context for LLMs

## 🎯 Mission
AIOptimize by Thatworkx is an Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) solution that helps structure your content. It ensures that Answer Engines like Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity can correctly understand, decode, and cite your content for the most relevant responses.

## 👥 Target Audience
- **Content Creators:** Bloggers, influencers, and researchers.
- **Marketing Professionals:** Marketing leadership, product marketers, and agency specialists.
- **Web Developers:** Web asset creators and technical SEO specialists.
- **Anyone** who wants to understand how web and mobile assets perform in an Answer Engine environment.

## 🛠 Technical Specifications & Analyzers
AIOptimize uses five core analyzers to evaluate page performance:
1. **Technical Foundation:** Evaluates schema markup, crawlability, and HTML structure.
2. **Content Structure:** Measures machine readability, Q&A patterns, and factual density.
3. **Page-Level E-E-A-T:** Checks for experience, expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness signals.
4. **Query Match Analysis:** Evaluates keyword presence and semantic relevance.
5. **AI Visibility Assessment:** Predicts citation potential and structural accessibility.
